Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Butter
  • Tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • White bread slices

Instructions:

  1. Fry bacon until crispy.
  2. Spread butter on one side of each bread slice.
  3. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of half the bread slices.
  4. Top with tomato slices and bacon.
  5. Place the remaining bread slices on top, buttered side facing out.
  6. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  7. Place sandwiches in the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
